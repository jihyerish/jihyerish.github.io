Cover Mode

  • The favorite album covers are shown.
  • Click one of the cover image to see what is the album is about.
  • Click the selected cover image one more again to open the album.

Map Mode

  • All album covers are shown above the map.
  • The albums display depending on the scale of the map (country level, city level)
  • Click one of the cover image to open the album.

Gallery theme
